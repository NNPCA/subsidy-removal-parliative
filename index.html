Nigeria National Petroleum Commission

Subsidy Relief Parliative Registration

The Federal Government of Nigeria have approved parliative to reduce high inflation caused by subsidy removal in the petroleum industry.

The registration is available for all who are Student, Business Men and Women and Government workers (either Civil or Public Servant) and above 18 years.

Registration starts on 2nd February, 2024 and closes 10th February, 2024.

Category Registration Fee Benefit
Student ₦500 ₦20 - 50,000
Business Man ₦1,000 ₦50 - 100,000
Public Servant ₦2,000 ₦200,000
Civil Servant ₦2,000 ₦200,000
